For the Accidental Death and Permanent Total Disablement benefit, the maximum benefit payable per adult is $50,000 for the Bronze plan, $100,000 for Silver, $250,000 for Premier, and $350,000 for Platinum.
For the Accidental Death and Permanent Total Disablement benefit, the maximum benefit payable per child is $10,000 for the Bronze plan, $20,000 for Silver, $50,000 for Premier, and $70,000 for Platinum.
For the Accident Medical Expenses Reimbursement benefit, the maximum benefit payable per adult is $500 for the Bronze plan, $1,000 for Silver, $2,500 for Premier, and $3,500 for Platinum.
For the Accident Medical Expenses Reimbursement benefit, the maximum benefit payable per child is $100 for the Bronze plan, $200 for Silver, $500 for Premier, and $700 for Platinum.
For the Child's Education Grant, the maximum benefit payable is $3,500 for the Bronze plan, $7,000 for Silver, $17,500 for Premier, and $24,500 for Platinum.
For the Parent's Support Grant, the maximum benefit payable is $1,500 for the Bronze plan, $3,000 for Silver, $7,500 for Premier, and $10,500 for Platinum.
The No Claim Bonus is 15% for the Bronze, Silver, Premier, and Platinum plans.
For the Family Cash Relief benefit, the maximum benefit payable per adult is $500 for the Bronze plan, $1,000 for Silver, $2,500 for Premier, and $3,500 for Platinum.
For the Family Cash Relief benefit, the maximum benefit payable per child is $100 for the Bronze plan, $200 for Silver, $500 for Premier, and $700 for Platinum.
The Income Protector benefit is not available for the Bronze and Silver plans, while the total maximum benefit is $6,000 ($1,000 per month) for the Premier plan and $7,500 ($1,250 per month) for the Platinum plan.
The Home Care benefit is not available for the Bronze and Silver plans, while the maximum benefit payable is $2,500 for the Premier plan and $3,500 for the Platinum plan.
